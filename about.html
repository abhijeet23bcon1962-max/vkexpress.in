Our Story

Serving India Since 2000

V. K. Express Courier & Cargo started its operation in year 2000, with a vision to be different from the other courier companies. Company has a long term plan and a vision of being expertise has heaped us gain creditability.

We V. K. Express Couriers are one of the fast growing courier companies dealing in Local, domestic & international couriers. We do have a wide network related to the International & Domestic set up. We are also a renewed services provider for the domestic cargo.

Our aim is to provide a professional and courteous service by ensuring Hi-Speed Services Ltd is the preferred choice of customers, achieved through the first class service, teamwork and excellent communications.

Our service is designed to provide you with complete peace of mind and eliminate the worries associated with time critical deliveries. Our service is available 24 hours a day, everyday.
